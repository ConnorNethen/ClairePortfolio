About Me

Claire Hanel is a native of Milwaukee Wisconsin. She grew up drawing since kindergarten, thus kickstarting her love of art all the way through college. All her life she has loved movies and tv-shows, wanting to know how they were created, the behind-the-scenes. This led her to get a BA in animation at UW-Milwaukee. She is a 2D illustrator and animator. Her current project is illustrating a book with a coworker.

Artist Statement

My art is an invitation to a whimsical universe, a fantastical gallery of illustrations and animations that defy the mundane and embrace the extraordinary and quirky. Each piece is a portal to a world where the weird is wonderful. My creations range from otherworldly landscapes to fluid animations, each a celebration of the peculiar and the sublime.

I like working with different media, preferably watercolor, gouache, ink pens, acrylic, and digital programs. With these different mediums, I am able to be meticulous yet fluid, which allows for free-spirited creations. It's a bit like a planned adventure where the path might shift but the destination is always somewhere fantastic.

I create, animate, and try to connect with those who find joy in the escape of reality. I want my art to be fun and relatable. In sharing my vision, I hope to inspire others to embrace their own creativity, to explore their imagination, and to celebrate the oddities that make our perspectives uniquely beautiful.
